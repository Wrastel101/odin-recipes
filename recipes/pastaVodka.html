Pasta Alla Vodka Recipe


Great Dinner for your significant other

My girlfriend loves this dish! She gets excited everytime I even mention it!

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Pasta of Choice
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Chili Flakes
  • Basil
  • Tomato Paste
  • Onions
  • Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Mozzerela Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil your pasta, save a cup of pasta water
  2. To a pan, add oil and throw in your garlic
  3. When the garlic starts goldening, throw in your onions, and cook them down
  4. As onions are softening, toss in your tomato pasta and mix around
  5. Add in all of your seasonings to the pan and stir them into the paste
  6. Add heavy whipping cream and your pasta water and mix
  7. Throw in your cooked pasta, along with parmesan cheese, and mozzerela cheese
  8. Mix until cheese is melted
  9. Serve with basil as a garnish
  10. Enjoy!
