Fried Rice Recipe


Great for easy dinners!

Excellent Asian inspired Fried Rice recipe that is fast and can feed many.

Ingredients

  • Day old rice
  • Carrots
  • Peas
  • Chicken (optional)
  • Eggs
  • Soy Sauce
  • Chicken Buyon
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chili Flakes

Steps

  1. Oil your pan with a high heat oil
  2. Throw in your day old rice and break apart the rice
  3. Add in all of your seasonings and soy sauce into the pan and mix
  4. Add all of the veggies into the pan with a dash of salt and mix
  5. Add in eggs and cook alongside the other ingredients
  6. Add your cooked chicke
  7. Enjoy!
